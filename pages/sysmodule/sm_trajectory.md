## Overview

### Trajectory view

* Cells selection
    * Cells of interest are selected and highlighted after clicking those.
    * Then, relevant lineages are also highlighted in the Lineage view.
* Zoom in and out
    * Region can be zoomed in and out using mouse scrolling.
